# Breakfast Sandwiches
### Ingredients:
- 4 English muffins, split and toasted
- 4 large eggs
- 4 slices of cheese
- 4 slices of cooked bacon or sausage patties

### Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Place English muffin halves on a baking sheet.
3. Top each half with a slice of cheese and a slice of bacon or sausage.
4. Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly.
5. Meanwhile, fry eggs to desired doneness.
6. Assemble sandwiches with egg and top with the other muffin half.
7. Wrap in foil and store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
8. Reheat in the microwave before serving.
